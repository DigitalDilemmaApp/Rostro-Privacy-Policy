Privacy Policy

Rostro · Digital Dilemma · Last updated: 5 May 2026

Rostro is a Windows desktop application built for teachers to track learner book checks, attendance, and class concerns. This policy explains how your data is handled.

1. Data Collection

Rostro does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information you enter — including learner names, class details, attendance records, and session notes — is stored exclusively on your local device.

2. Local Storage Only

All app data is saved in a local SQLite database on your computer. No data is sent to any server, cloud service, or third party at any time.

3. No Internet Connection Required

Rostro works entirely offline. The app does not make any network requests, and no internet connection is needed or used during normal operation.

4. No Analytics or Tracking

Rostro does not include any analytics, crash reporting, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools of any kind.

5. Children's Data

Rostro is used by teachers to manage classroom data. Any learner information entered into the app remains solely on the teacher's device and is never transmitted externally. Digital Dilemma has no access to this data.

6. Data Security

Because all data remains on your local device, you are responsible for the security of your computer. We recommend standard device security practices such as screen locking and regular backups.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in a future version of Rostro, the updated policy will be published at this URL and reflected in the app's Store listing.
